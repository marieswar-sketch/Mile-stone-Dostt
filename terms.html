Locked Wallet - Terms & Conditions

1. Bonus Coins

50% extra coins are credited on every recharge to the Locked Wallet.

2. Locked Wallet

Bonus coins are stored separately in your Locked Wallet and remain locked until unlocked through usage.

3. Unlocking Rule

For every 4 coins spent on Dostt calls, 1 bonus coin unlocks from your Locked Wallet.

4. Expiry

Bonus coins expire 30 days from credit date. Unused locked or unlocked bonus coins are forfeited.

5. Usage Scope

Bonus coins are applicable only for Dostt calls.

6. Restrictions

Locked coins cannot be withdrawn, transferred, or converted to cash.

7. Multiple Recharges

Each recharge creates a separate locked-bonus batch with its own 30-day expiry cycle.

8. Platform Rights

Dostt may modify, suspend, or discontinue the Locked Wallet feature at any time.
